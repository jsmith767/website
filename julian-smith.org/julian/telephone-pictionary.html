Telephone Pictionary is a cross between the classic game of Telephone and Pictionary.
Players alternate writing phrases and drawing pictures that describe those phrases.
Just like in the classic game of Telephone the message starts to get lost!
I've created a very basic online version here for safe play during social distancing.

Instructions

  1. Receive a prompt: Either a drawing or a phrase.
    Also included is a game name and player number.
  2. If you received a drawing: write a phrase to describe the picture.
    If you received a phrase: draw a picture to interpret the phrase.
  3. Once you have finalized you response...
    ...Go to your game link and upload a picture of your response (sign your work).
    Name the file with your player# (e.g. 002).
  4. Look through the past responses.
  5. Send your response to another person who hasn't received it yet!
    Include the game name, their player# (your player# + 1), and a link to this page.
Don't look at other games unless explicitely told to! You might ruin the fun for yourself if it just hasn't reached you yet!
If you have any questions or would like to start a game yourself feel free to email me at jsmith767@gmail.com
