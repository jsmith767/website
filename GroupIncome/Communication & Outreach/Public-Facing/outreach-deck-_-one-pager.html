Outreach Deck One Pager

Group Income Program — “One Pager”

Vision:

"Ensuring economic stability, empowering communities, and fostering collaboration through shared prosperity."


What We Do

Provide a stable, livable income baseline.

Operate as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit research/education program.

Enable optional affinity groups for deeper sharing and community projects.

Support global participants with PPP-adjusted payouts.

Transparent program finances; personal privacy protected.


How It Works

Contribute: Base % of income (e.g., 10%) to shared pool.

Receive: Equitable payouts; all participants get the same base.

Affinity Groups: Optional extra sharing within trusted groups.

Debt & Wealth Adjustments: Contributions account for debt; windfalls taxed as wealth, not income.

Safety Net: Buffer fund ensures stability and emergency coverage.


Participation

Open to eligible individuals agreeing to program rules.

Exit & Re-entry: Leave anytime; rejoin with structured ramp-up (1% initial contribution for first months per re-entry).

Annual Renewal: Membership agreements aligned with tax year.


Core Principles

Equity – Access to basic economic security.

Transparency – Open finances and decision-making.

Mutual Care – Protect one another from sudden loss.

Freedom – Economic stability enables autonomy and creativity.


Why It Matters

Supports irregular income earners (artists, freelancers, gig workers).

Provides economic freedom and safety from harmful systems.

Creates a platform for flexible income-sharing groups.

Contributes to research and innovation in social and economic systems.


Quick Stats

Base Contribution: 10% of Effective Income

Buffer Fund: 5%

Affinity Groups: Optional extra sharing

Global Reach: PPP-adjusted payouts
